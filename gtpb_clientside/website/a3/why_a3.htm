Strategic issue:Lean Service Organisations and A3 online reports
1) Why look at this problem, now? (Urgency review)
The Lean community is buzzing about A3 reports. Take a look at Jim Womack's 2008 speech to the Lean Management Summit in India.
2) What's the situation now? (Analysis)
Most consultants recognise the importance of Lean, however a survey by LEI shows widespread concern about embedding it in organisations, with 48% of respondents reporting "backsliding into old ways of working". In manufacturing the A3 process is coming to be seen as part of the solution to this issue.
3) Where do we want to be? (Desired future state)
An A3 process that fits the service sector environment
4) Why is the service sector in this position? (Root cause analysis)
In the service sector paper based A3 reports do not work well as teams often work
- from a number of offices, making collaboration difficult
- in open plan offices or cubicles lacking wall space to display finished reports (a critical part of the process)
- in areas the public have access to, which can lead to privacy concerns
- in "front offices" that need to project a positive brand / image that A3s need to fit in with
Progress checking often becomes an issue without a single up to date version of the report.
5) What do we need to do? (Countermeasures)
Pilot this online A3 system that
- enables staff to collaborate regardless of their location
- enables individuals who lack dedicated wall space access a library of reports
- a uniform look and feel to support a company image
- ability to tailor the reports to our organisational language
