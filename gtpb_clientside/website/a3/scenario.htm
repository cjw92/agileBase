Strategic issue:Why use an online A3 report tool? Report ID:2 By:Cliff Date:28th July 2010
1) Why are we looking at this problem, now? (Urgency review)
Our second office has just opened in Brazil. We need to ensure our hard won acceptance of the value of A3 reports for decision-making is not lost.
2) Where are we now? (Situational analysis)
We use paper A3 reports across the entire London office. Reports are shared on dedicated wall space to build consensus. Unfortunately, non office based staff are often left out of key decisions and we have tended to ignore this.
3) Where do we want to be? (Desired future state)
All the value of A3 planning etc is shared with both non office staff and with the new staff in Brazil.
4) Why are we where we are now? (Root cause analysis)
In the past our staff were largely based in the same office so visual management i.e. using wall space, has worked well enough. The number of reports was manageable as only one version really mattered (the one on the wall)
5) What do we need to do? (Countermeasures)
Trial www.a3reports.co.uk to see if it allows us to collaborate in the creation process.
6) How will we know when it has been achieved? (Measures, metrics and description of what will suffice?)
Day 1 - Setup 30 day free trial
Day 15 - Findings reported to senior management team
Day 30 - Agreement (or rejection) received.
7) How can we ensure changes are embedded? (SOP)
Give access to "lite" version of tool to all staff
8) Follow up actions to be taken (Lessons learned)
Investigate "pro" version, depending on outcome of trial
